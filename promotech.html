Hydraulic Punchers

ATEX Drills for Hazardous Environments

ATEX drill family conform to Ex c IIC T6/T4 Gb and Ex c IIIB T6/T4 Dd requirements for equipment used in hazardous environments such as oil drilling platforms, petrochemical plants, oil refineries, shipbuilding and petrochemical industry, where electric appliances are prohibited for safety reasons. Combination of pneumatic drive and permanent magnetic base eliminate the need of electric power supply allowing for a safe use in Ex zones and where hazardous, flammable or combustible vapors, liquids, dusts or residues may be present.
