About RootsScrub E4043

  • Handy, mains operated scrubber-drier designed for quick, economical, and effective cleaning.

  • Robust, compact construction guarantees exceptional maneuverability and easy handling.

  • Features low running costs, unmatched cleanliness, and simple maintenance procedures.

  • Variety of brushes and pads available to suit any hard floor condition.

Key Design Features

Intuitive Operation

All operational switches are located within direct view and easy reach, providing the operator an excellent view of the cleaning area.

Hassle-Free Cleaning

Tank cleaning is simplified thanks to a large opening, and emptying is quick and easy via the generous outlet hose.

Protected Vacuum

A powerful suction motor is protected by a filter and sensor, ensuring the vacuum motor remains safe even when the dirty water tank is full.

Quick Brush Swap

Changing brushes is an effortless task that can be completed quickly with the push of a dedicated button.
