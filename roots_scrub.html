About RootsScrub E4043

The RootsScrub E4043 machine is a handy, mains operated scrubber-drier for quick, economical and effective cleaning of all types of hard floors. The extremely compact and robustly constructed machine guarantees high degree of maneuverability, easy handling and good visibility. RootsScrub E4043 offers low running costs, optimum cleanliness and ease of maintenance.

Variety of brushes and pads are available to suit different floor conditions.

Key Features

Easy Operation

All operational switches are within direct view and easy reach of the operator. Excellent view of the area to be cleaned.

Simple Tank Cleaning

Easy tank cleaning, thanks to the large tank opening. Tank can be emptied quickly and easily via the large outlet hose.

Powerful Suction

Powerful suction motor with filter protection and sensor ensures safety of vacuum motor in the dirty water tank.

Effortless Brush Change

Brushes can be effortlessly changed quickly at the push of a button.
