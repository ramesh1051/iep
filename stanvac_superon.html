I. MAINTENANCE & REPAIR WELDING

Product Flow Breakdown

Why Superon M&R Alloys:

  • Assured 10-15% longer life, both in joining & in hardfacing.

  • Lowest in class heat input (Shorter arc gaps-touch welding) & lower input amps.

  • Lower dilution results in superior weld metal chemistries and better mechanical properties.

  • Lesser possibilities of HAZ cracking and base metal weakening.

  • Cl repairs are crack and defect free, even on dirty & contaminated base metal.

  • Tubular hardfacing features 4G capabilities, no red hotness, minimum dilution, and maximum deposit efficiency.
